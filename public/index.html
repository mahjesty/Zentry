Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Zentry Banking.

How do I open an account?

Opening an account is simple. Click on "Open Account" and follow our 5-minute application process. You'll need to provide identification and complete a quick verification.

Is my money safe with Zentry?

Absolutely. Zentry Banking is FDIC insured up to $250,000 per depositor. We also employ bank-grade security measures including encryption, fraud monitoring, and multi-factor authentication.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. We believe in complete transparency. All fees are clearly outlined in our pricing section and during the account opening process. There are no surprise charges.

How quickly can I access my funds?

With Zentry, you have immediate access to deposited funds. Transfers between Zentry accounts happen instantly, and external transfers typically complete within 1-2 business days.

Can I use my card internationally?

Yes. Zentry cards work worldwide with competitive exchange rates and no foreign transaction fees on Premium and Business accounts.

What customer support options are available?

We offer 24/7 customer support via in-app chat, email, and phone. Premium and Business customers also have access to dedicated support representatives.
